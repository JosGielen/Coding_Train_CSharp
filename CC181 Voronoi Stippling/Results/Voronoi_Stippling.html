Weighted Voronoi Stippling

This program was made in C#. It uses the Voronator-Sharp C# library made by BorisTheBrave to calculate the Delaunay Triangulation and the Voronoi diagram.

The following images show the different steps in the construction of a Voronoi diagram

 Generate some random seed points.      Calculate the Delaunay Triangulation.      Generate the Voronoi Polygons from the triangle circumcircle centers
             

Relaxing the Voronoi Diagram

The Voronoi diagram can be relaxed by moving the Seed Points towards the centers of each polygon.
